# Boolean algebra

| **L**   | **H**   |
| --- | --- |

## Identity law
$$
\begin{align}
	&x+0=0&x*0=0\\
	&x+1=1&x*1=1\\
\end{align}
$$

## Associative law
$$
\begin{align}
	&(a+b)+c=a+(b+c)\\
	&(ab)c=a(bc)\\
\end{align}
$$

## Distributive law
$$
\begin{align}
	&(a+b)c=ac+bc\\
	&a+bc=(a+b)(a+c)\\
\end{align}
$$

## Absorption law
$$
\begin{align}
	&x+xy=x&x(x+y)=x\\
	&x*1=xy=x(1+y)=x&x*x+xy=x+xy\\
\end{align}
$$
