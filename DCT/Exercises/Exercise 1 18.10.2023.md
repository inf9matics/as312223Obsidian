## Identity law
$$
\begin{align}
	&x+0=0&x*0=0\\
	&x+1=1&x*1=1\\
\end{align}
$$

## Associative law
$$
\begin{align}
	&(a+b)+c=a+(b+c)\\
	&(ab)c=a(bc)\\
\end{align}
$$

## Distributive law
$$
\begin{align}
	&(a+b)c=ac+bc\\
	&a+bc=(a+b)(a+c)\\
\end{align}
$$

## Absorption law
$$
\begin{align}
	&1.&&x+xy=x&&x(x+y)=x\\
	&&&x*1=xy=x(1+y)=x&&x*x+xy=x+xy\\
	&\\
	&2.&&x+\overline{x}y=x+y&&x(\overline{x}+y)=xy\\
	&&&x+\overline{x}y=(x+\overline{x})(x+y)=x+y&&x\overline{x}+xy=xy\\
\end{align}
$$

## De Morgan's law
$$
\begin{align}
	&\overline{a+b}=\overline{a}+\overline{b}\\
	&\overline{ab}=\overline{a}+\overline{b}\\
\end{align}
$$
