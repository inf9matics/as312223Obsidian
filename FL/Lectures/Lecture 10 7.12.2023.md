# Irregular verb conjugation\*
## "g" in the first person
### List
- hacer
	- ha**g**o
	- haces
	- hace
	- hacemos
	- hacéis
	- hacen
- salir
	- sal**g**o
	- sales
	- sale
	- salimos
	- salís
	- salen

## Vowel replacement
### Rule
The vowels are replaced in all singular forms and the third plural form.

### List
- jugar
	- ju**e**go
	- ju**e**gas
	- ju**e**ga
	- jugamos
	- jugáis
	- ju**e**gan
- cerrar
	- c**i**erro
	- c**i**erras
	- c**i**erra
	- cerramos
	- cerráis
	- c**i**erran

## Verb with "g" and changing "-e" to "-ie"
### Rule
The vowels are replaced the second and third singular form and the third plural form.

### List
- tenner
	- ten**g**o
	- t**ie**nes
	- t**ie**ne
	- tenemos
	- tenéis
	- t**ie**nen
