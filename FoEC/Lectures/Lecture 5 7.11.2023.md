# Separation principle (Source Substitution Theorem)
## Applications
- Calculation of power transferred between subcircuits
- Independent analyses of subcircuits

## Power balance formula
$$
\begin{align}
	&P_A=P_{U_1}+P_{U_2}=I_1U_1+I_2U_2\\
\end{align}
$$
