# Formulas
## Height of free fall
$$
\begin{align}
	&h=\frac{gt^2}{2}\\
\end{align}
$$

## Speed
$$
\begin{align}
	&v_t=\sqrt{v_x^2+v_y^2}\\
\end{align}
$$
## Time of falling with initial upward speed v at height of d
$$
\begin{align}
	&t=\frac{v_0}{a}\sqrt{\frac{2d}{a}}\\
\end{align}
$$
