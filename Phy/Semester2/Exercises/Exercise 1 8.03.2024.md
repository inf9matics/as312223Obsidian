## Problem no. 10
$$
\begin{align}
	&\vec a=[1,4,3]\\
	&\vec b=[-3,4,2]\\
	&\vec a*\vec b=-3+16+6=19\\
	&\theta=\angle{\vec a}{\vec b}\\
	&\cos{\theta}=\frac{\vec a*\vec b}{|\vec a||\vec b|}\approx\frac{19}{27.45906}\approx0.691939\\
	&\theta\approx\arccos{0.691939}\approx46.216205\degree=0.8066[rad]\\
\end{align}
$$
